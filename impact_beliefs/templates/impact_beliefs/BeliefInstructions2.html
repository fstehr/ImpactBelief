Task 4 - Instructions

In this task, you are again asked to estimate the number of Xs in a matrix. As in Task 2, the Xs stand for the amount of CO2 emissions you can eliminate through the Donation Task in Task 5. As before, 1 X in the matrix represents {{ Constants.real_world_kg_co2_per_x }} kg of CO2.

You only get to see the matrix for {{ Constants.sec_per_matrix }} seconds. After that, you have {{ Constants.sec_to_answer }} seconds to fill in your estimate of the number of X's seen. If you fail to submit your estimate on time more than twice, your payments will be forfeited.

Your bonus payment in this task will be determined as in Task 2:

You will receive {{ Constants.beliefs_fixed_payment }} Points for completing the task on time if one round of this task is selected for payment. Depending on the accuracy of your estimate, you can earn up to {{ Constants.beliefs_max_accuracy_bonus }} points more. In particular, you earn more the closer you are to the correct number of Xs.
