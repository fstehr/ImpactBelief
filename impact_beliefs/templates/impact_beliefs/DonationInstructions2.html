Task 5 - Instructions

In this task, you are again given the opportunity to donate some of your earnings from your work in Task 1. For each decision, we will show you one project. You will be informed of the price of the donation to this project. You will also be informed of {% if player.treatment == "Info" %} the true number of Xs in the matrix, and the true amount of CO2 emissions that are eliminated if you decide to donate to this project. {% else %} your estimate of how much CO2 emissions will be eliminated through this project, so your estimate on the number of Xs of this project in the previous task. {% endif %}

If you decide to donate to a project in a given round and this round is selected for payment, we will donate the amount to climatecare.org, a company that eliminates CO2 emissions from the atmosphere. Thus, through your donation real CO2 emissions in the atmosphere will be reduced .
