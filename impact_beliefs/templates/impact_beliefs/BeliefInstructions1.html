Your Bonus Payments

Your bonus payment in this task

For completing the task, you will receive {{ Constants.beliefs_fixed_payment }} Points, if a round of this task is selected for payment. You can earn more money depending on the accuracy of your estimate. In particular, you earn more the closer you are to the correct number of Xs. If you get the correct number of Xs, you earn {{ Constants.beliefs_max_payment }} Points. The further your estimate is away from the correct number of Xs, the less you earn. If you miss the number of Xs by more than 200 your extra payoff for accuracy in this task will be zero.

Your specific earnings are based on a mathematical formula. It is not necessary for you to precisely understand the formulas, but it is important that you understand that you earn more the closer your estimate is to the true number of Xs in a matrix. Importantly, it does not make a difference whether you over- or underestimate the true number of Xs. It only matters by how much you are away from the true number of Xs. If you would like to see the precise mathematical formula, you can click here.

Comprehension Questions

Bonus Payment Formula

Your extra payments for accuracy in this task are determined by the following formula:

$$ {{ Constants.beliefs_fixed_payment }} - 0.00375 * \left( \text{Your estimate} - \text{true number of Xs} \right)^2 $$

This formula will become negative, if your estimate is further away from the true number of Xs than 200. If this should be the case, we will not subtract anything from your fixed bonus for this task. Instead, your extra earnings for accuracy will become zero.
