We apply the following procedure: 

  1. For each task we calculate the error you made. This is how many percentage points your report was away from the true answer.
  2. We square the error. 
  3. We calculate your accuracy: We subtract the squared error from 100%. 
  4. We find the reward to this task: We multiply by 5.
  5. Then, we randomly pick one of the tasks, your bonus will be equal to the reward for this task.

----------------------------------

EXAMPLE: Suppose that you report 80% that a RED BOX was picked in Step 1. Then, your rewards are as follows:
  • If the BOX was indeed RED:
  • your error is (100%-80%)=20%,
  • your squared error is (20%)*(20%)=4%
  • your accuracy is 100%-4%=96%
